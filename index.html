About Us

Kiddies Corner High School, Imphal is an English medium Co-educational institution. It admits children irrespective of caste or creed. It was established in 1993 at Chingmeirong. It is own and solely managed by the Kiddies Corner Residential Society. The school provides a sound atmosphere where learning is imparted with tender consideration. At the Kindergarten level it is a playground of learning with timely correction and guidance. The school is located on the eastern side of Manipur Assembly

FROM THE PRINCIPAL’S DESK

Dear Parents/Guardians,

On behalf of the faculty and staff, I am pleased to welcome you to our Kiddies Corner High School website. We warmly welcome all of you who are new to our Kiddian family.

We, at Kiddies Corner High School are determined to provide the best educational environment and resources to encourage student participation in every field of learning and also increase student performance and achievement. We strive to stand true to the role you have entrusted us with, to create lifelong learners and we hope that with your support we can foster academic success. We also appeal to you to become involved in your child’s activities, as you are an important element in creating a successful scholastic year.

We will do our best to ensure that every student receive the services and care for a positive educational experience. All of our teachers are highly qualified and most dedicated and professional in their approach to teaching. The longevity of the staff also speaks to their dedication and commitment beyond the school. We at Kiddies Corner High School follow a dynamic program that continually challenge each student and staff to develop and enhance skills and talents in support of one another, and all those who may be in need. The diversity of the student population also creates unique opportunities that foster a true team approach in developing highly individualized educational programs seeking to empower each student to use their strengths to help other.

In addition to a strong framework of structure and routine in our school, there is also a familial atmosphere of genuine warmth and happiness that floats through the interaction between staff, students and families. We embrace all those who enter in a spirit of mutual respect, collaboration and advocacy toward hope and possibility for a lifetime. If you ever have concerns and questions, please call the school office between the office hours of 9:00 am to 2:55 pm

Thank you for sharing your child with us. We look forward to great sessions ahead filled with positive learning experiences.

Sincerely,
Mrs. Cecilia Vanphong
Principal

Our Vision

The aim of the school is to impart sound education by cultivating in the students habits of piety, virtue, self-reliance and discipline, while they go through their studies. Hence particular attention is given to discipline and character formation by means of religious and moral instruction

In order to achieve its educational goals, the school is divided into four sections:
Kindergarten : Pre Nursery to UKG
Junior School : Class 1 – V
Middle School : Class VI – VIII
High School : Class IX & X
