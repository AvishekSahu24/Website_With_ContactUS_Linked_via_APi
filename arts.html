ARTS & CRAFTS

Art is a means of communication. Art and crafts offers the range of activities for children, a wide variety of activities involving making things with one’s own hand. Children in KCHS may learn skills such as Bamboo carving, sewing, bottle painting and making things with all sorts of waste materials. Students are guided in creatively applying knowledge and skills to every type of design.
